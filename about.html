About Me

My experience balancing academics, coaching, and competitive chess has taught me the value of discipline, strategy, and perseverance.

I'm passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and I'm especially drawn to data-driven decision-making, software development, and accessibility solution. I also mentor visually impaired students in chess, helping make learning inclusive and empowering through tailored strategies.

My mission is to merge my analytical thinking and technical knowledge with compassion and creativity to make a lasting, positive impact - in tech, in education, and beyond.
